This is Robert Whittaker

Robert Whittaker, also known as "The Reaper" is a New-Zealand-born Australian professional mixed martial artist who is currently the #1 ranked middleweight in the UFC. Prior to October of last year Whittaker was the UFC Middleweight World Champion.


As of today he holds a professional record of 20 wins and 5 losses. 8 of those wins are finishes by a KO(Knockout) or a TKO(Technical Knockout).


He is one of my favorite MMA fighters of all time because he has a very exciting style of fighting and he is a good person outside of the octagon. He also had an impressive 8 fight win streak on his way to the title.

Championships and accomplishments

  1. Winner of Australia Cup Wrestling
  2. UFC Middleweight Champion
  3. 2018 GQ Australia Sportsman of the year

Fighting acolytes

  • Black belt in Hapkido
  • Black belt in Goju-ryu Karate
  • Brown belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu
